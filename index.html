FAQ
How do I recharge?

Go to Recharge, enter amount, send via Telebirr to 0956899452, then enter your Transaction ID to verify and add balance.

Why is my transaction rejected?

If you see "Transaction ID already used", it means this ID has been submitted before. Each Telebirr transaction generates a unique ID. Make sure you're entering the correct one.

Why are tasks locked?

Tasks require at least one VIP product purchase. Balance from recharge alone does not unlock tasks.

When are withdrawals processed?

3 daily slots: 09:00-11:00, 13:00-15:00, 17:00-19:00. No Sundays.

What is the withdrawal fee?

15% service fee. Withdraw ETB 1,000, receive ETB 850.
